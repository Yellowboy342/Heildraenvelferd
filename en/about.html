About Heildrænvelferð

A holistic approach to health, wellness, and inner peace

Heildrænvelferð is the massage studio of Sigrún Heiða Birgisdóttir — Siddí — in the heart of Reykjavík. We emphasize a holistic approach where body, mind, and soul receive care and attention.

We believe that health is more than physical wellness. It encompasses mental balance, emotional tranquility, and the connection to one's inner self. Massage is one of the most powerful tools for strengthening these connections and promoting deep wellbeing.

Our studio is a peaceful, warm sanctuary where you can set aside your worries and focus on your own wellness. Whether you come to us for pain, stress, or simply to enjoy a moment of peace — we are here for you.

Our Philosophy

The lotus flower is our symbol — it grows from the mud and blooms in beauty and purity

Body

We work with the body in a deep and respectful way. Using different massage techniques, we address each person's needs and help the body release tension, work through pain, and restore balance.

Mind

Massage is not just a physical treatment — it is also a moment where the mind gets rest. In our treatments, we create a space where stress fades and inner peace takes over.

Soul

Holistic wellness is about listening to yourself. We believe that when body and mind are in balance, inner peace flourishes and the soul gets space to recover and grow.
