About Heildrænvelferð

A holistic approach to health and wellbeing

Heildrænvelferð is the massage studio of Sigrún Heiða Birgisdóttir (Siddí) in Reykjavík. We emphasize a holistic approach to wellness — where body, mind, and soul receive attention and care.

We believe that massage is not just a treatment, but an investment in your own health and wellbeing. Whether you're seeking relaxation, pain relief, or renewal, we're here for you.
